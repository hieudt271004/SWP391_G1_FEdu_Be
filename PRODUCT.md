# Product

## Register

product

## Users
- **Admin**: System administrators who configure users, roles, courses, and classes. They need a fast, high-density, authoritative command center.
- **Teacher**: Instructors managing learning paths, uploading materials, grading tests, and communicating with students.
- **Student**: Learners who access learning nodes, watch videos, read materials, take quizzes, and track their progress.
- **Sub-Mentor**: Tutors answering support tickets and helping students resolve academic queries.

## Product Purpose
FEdu is a Learning Management System (LMS) built to connect students, teachers, mentors, and administrators. It streamlines education management and coursework progress tracking. Success for the Admin Dashboard looks like an extremely clean, readable, professional command center that presents key system metrics and quick management actions clearly.

## Brand Personality
- **Authoritative & Professional**: Instills trust in educational operations.
- **Clean & High-Density**: Displays rich information without feeling cramped or overwhelming.
- **Modern Minimalist**: Leans toward crisp typography (Outfit/sans-serif), high contrast, and structured grid layouts.

## Anti-references
- **AI-Slop Clichés**: Excessive card rounding (>12-16px), floating glassmorphism overlays, decorative colored card side-borders, decorative text gradients, and low-contrast muted-gray text.
- **Vague Metrics**: Card layouts that lack visual priority, clear hierarchy, or real utility.

## Design Principles
1. **Density with Intent**: Place data close together to allow rapid scanning, but use strict grid alignments and spacing rules to maintain breathing room.
2. **Text Contrast First**: Ensure all body text, states, and indicators have high contrast against backgrounds.
3. **Semantic Hierarchy**: Differentiate sections, buttons, and status labels by size, weight, and subtle colors rather than flashy decorations.
4. **Immediate Utility**: Action buttons and navigation links must be prominent and visually clean.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Follow WCAG 2.1 AA contrast ratios (minimum 4.5:1 for body and normal text).
- Responsive layouts with clear flex/grid flows that work well on desktop, tablet, and mobile.
- Clear hover/focus outlines and visible tap targets for keyboard and screen reader access.
